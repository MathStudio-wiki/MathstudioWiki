## TrigConvert(expression)

将三角函数转换为指数函数。


## 举例  
[在Mathstudio上浏览](http://mathstud.io/?input[0]=VHJpZ0NvbnZlcnQoc2luKHgpKQ%3D%3D&input[1]=VHJpZ0NvbnZlcnQoY29zKHgpKQ%3D%3D&input[2]=VHJpZ0NvbnZlcnQodGFuKHgpKQ%3D%3D&input[3]=VHJpZ0NvbnZlcnQoY290KHgpKQ%3D%3D)





>   ```math
>   TrigConvert(sin(x))
>   ```
>   $ \frac{1}{2} i e^{-i x}-\frac{1}{2} i e^{i x} $



>   ```math
>   TrigConvert(cos(x))
>   ```
>   $ \frac{1}{2} e^{i x}+\frac{1}{2} e^{-i x} $


>   ```math
>   TrigConvert(tan(x))
>   ```
>   $ \frac{\frac{1}{2} \mathrm{i} e^{-\mathrm{i} x}-\frac{1}{2} \mathrm{i} e^{i x}}{\frac{1}{2} e^{i x+\frac{1}{2} e^{-i x}}} $


>   ```math
>   TrigConvert(cot(x))
>   ```
>   $ \frac{\frac{1}{2} e^{i x}+\frac{1}{2} e^{-i x}}{\frac{1}{2} i e^{-i x}-\frac{1}{2} i e^{i x}} $
